# Privacy Policy — DVM Finance

_Last updated: 2026-07-17_

DVM Finance ("the app") is a personal-finance app that runs entirely on your
device. Your privacy is simple to describe because the app is built not to
collect anything.

## What we collect

**Nothing.** DVM Finance does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any
personal or financial information. There are no user accounts, no sign-in, no
analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking.

## Where your data lives

All information you add — transactions, categories, rules, budgets, and
snapshots — is stored **only** in a local database on your iPhone or iPad. It
never leaves your device except through actions you explicitly take (for
example, exporting a snapshot file and choosing where to save or share it).

## Data you import

Any bank statement or transaction data you import is processed on-device and
stored locally. It is not uploaded to us or to any server.

## Backups

If you use iCloud Device Backup or a computer backup, your device — including
the app's local database — may be included in that backup according to your own
Apple/iCloud settings. That backup is governed by Apple's privacy policy, not
ours.

## Children's privacy

The app is rated 4+ and collects no data from anyone, including children.
